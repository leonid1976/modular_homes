Find out more about Tassie Prefab homes
TASSIE PREFAB HOMES – is proudly Australian owned and operated company. Our company has been established in the Hobart area for 10 years. During this time we have built a reputation with Master Builders Association, local councils and families as quality home builders who take pride in their workmanship.

TASSIE PREFAB HOMES – is a modern, flexible build system to provide compact living spaces suitable for modern living needs. By investing in our people, processes, systems and products we will maintain the highest levels of service, solutions and innovation. Each member of our team will act in a friendly, responsible and professional manner.
TASSIE PREFAB HOMES' manufacturing facility has been meticulously designed and tested to ensure each home is produced to exceptionally high standards. The benefit of our streamlined production system not only ensures quality, but an ability to continue producing high quality at scale. Our new state-of-the-art facility and highly trained workforce have the capacity to produce 150 homes a year and we plan to further scale production.

Currently located in Derwent Park, on the north side of Hobart in Tasmania, TASSIE PREFAB HOMES continues providing customers with a trouble-free building alternative, from selecting the perfect home designs to arranging all of the relevant approvals to installation on-site.
